Sketchers'
Circle

    Sketchers' Circle is a global community for urban sketchers, bringing together artists of all skill levels who are passionate about capturing the beauty of the world through on-location sketches. Founded to provide a platform for sharing experiences, techniques, and inspiration, the community fosters creativity and skill development beyond the studio. Members can showcase their work, engage in discussions about sketching techniques, and participate in virtual and in-person sketching events, creating a supportive network of artists united by their love for drawing.

Our Vision and Mission

Sketchers' Circle

To inspire and connect urban sketchers worldwide, fostering creativity and artistic expression through shared experiences and collaborative events.

To provide a vibrant platform for artists of all skill levels to showcase their work and engage in a supportive community dedicated to sketching.
